Aug., 02 2000

DESCRIPTION:

Weekday allows you to show the weekday of any
date from 1.1.0000 to 31.12.3000. Just type the
date into the three fields and click "Show".
You'll be shown the weekday of the date. The
program shows you today's date as default. To
end weekday just close the main window with
the cross in the upper right.

Weekday is freeware and MUST NOT be sold.
